My Adventures toward Mountains

published on 27 May, 2024

When my wedding dates were fixed, I was so happy. I barely realised , I just have one month to stay with my parents, waking up late and not being judged, talking loudly , shouting and fighting with my siblings, asking for money from dad without any hesitation, not cooking or doing any household works, being a lazyass, staying late at cousins, going shopping anytime with ammu and thousands and thousands things! Won’t be addressed as my father’s daughter but husband’s wife, this whole thing of changing guardian ship. Won’t wake up on the Eid day seeing my parents and siblings. Won’t clean my house on chand raat and go out with friends or cousins. Will have to live according to the new rules,mentality,economy,circumstances apart from my Abbus. Need to change the lifestyle that my parents gave just within a blink of eyes. Staying at my home for a long time will be illegal and unethical. It’s not like I’m the only girl who’s getting married in this whole world but Little did I realise,my life will be changed forever.
